We need your old clothes

In addition to providing clean clothes to our Mission guests, we also provide clothing to abuse victims who may have had to flee their homes without clothing or anything, families in the community who are without funds to purchase clothing, and victims of fire and/or flooding. Clothing is also sent to our Bargain Centers with all proceeds going directly to Old Savannah City Mission. Once clothing in the Bargain Centers have been rotated, they are sent to our warehouse where the surplus is baled, put on a container and sold. Proceeds derived from the baling go directly to Old Savannah City Mission.

This is a “win-win” for everyone. Donors win because they get rid of what they no longer use; they give to the less fortunate and get a tax- break in the process. The Mission wins because we have items to distribute locally to individuals in need and the overage can be sold to help generate revenues to feed the hungry and shelter the homeless. Absolutely no clothing donations go to waste.
